### Mandatory and Bonuses

- WASD to move, QE or Left/Right directional keys to rotate camera
- ESC to quit
- Collision
- Textures or colors for each sides (North, South, West, East) and Sky/Floor
- Screenshot
- Minimap
- Animated sprites
- Chasing enemies
- Killable enemies
- Health bar
- Moving camera with mouse
- SPACE to jump
- SHIFT to dash forward
- CMD to duck
